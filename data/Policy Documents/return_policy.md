# TechFlow Electronics Return Policy

## Return Windows

### Standard Return Policy
- Unopened devices: 30 days from purchase date
- Opened devices: 14 days from purchase date
- Accessories: 30 days from purchase date
- Smart home devices: 21 days from purchase date

### Customer Tier Benefits

#### Premium Customers (Total Spent >$1,000)
- Extended return window: 45 days for all products
- No restocking fees: Waived for all returns
- Free return shipping: Prepaid return labels provided
- Priority processing: Returns processed within 24 hours

#### Regular Customers (Total Spent $200-$999)
- Standard return windows as listed above
- Restocking fee: 15% on opened electronics (waived for defects)
- Return shipping: Customer pays return shipping costs
- Processing time: 3-5 business days

#### New Customers (Total Spent <$200)
- Standard return windows apply
- Restocking fee: 20% on opened electronics
- Return shipping: Customer responsibility
- Processing time: 5-7 business days

## Refund Calculations

### Full Refunds Available For:
- Products returned within return window in original condition
- Defective products regardless of return window
- Products that fail within first 48 hours of use
- Warranty-covered manufacturing defects

### Special Circumstances

#### Health & Safety Issues
- Immediate full refund regardless of return window
- No restocking fees applied
- Expedited processing within 24 hours

#### Accidental Purchases
- Full refund available if product unused and unopened
- Must be reported within 72 hours of purchase
- Automatic approval for agent-level processing

## Processing Times
- Credit card refunds: 3-5 business days
- Bank transfers: 5-7 business days
- PayPal refunds: 1-2 business days

Always prioritize customer satisfaction while maintaining policy consistency.
